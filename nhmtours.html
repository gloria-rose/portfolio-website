Overview

The NHM Tours app for mobile presents storylines through museum exhibits, engaging visitors in a new way. NHM Tours has an educational mission, providing additional interactive dimensions to museum experiences.

The app is designed for museum visitors, guiding them through digital tours step-by-step. Users can choose between a number of digital tours, navigate the museum map and scan QR codes of exhibit items of interest.

The main objectives of the NHM Tours Project included finding solutions how to present information in an interesting and captivating way, how to guide users through the museum towards specific exhibits and to create a barrier-free museum experience.

Project Aims

  • Create a museum app which is easy to navigate for first-time users, which is a coherent extension of the museum exhibits (in visual language and content) and which provides new dimensions to the museum experience
  • Promote barrier-free access by including wheelchair-accessible routes and transcripts of all audio files
  • Support user-driven exploration of exhibits outside of guided tours through QR-codes

Prototype Testing

3 user tests for iOS, 2 user tests for Android

  • Spacing of map titles was improved
  • Card appearance was adapted for maps
  • Duplicate texts was removed
  • Checkmarks turn to x when clicked
  • A logo was added in the header to link back to the start page
  • "Tours" was renamed to "Home" in the hamburger menu
  • The button "Guide" was renamed "Map Guide"
  • "Featured" was renamed to "Featured tour"
  • The tops of cards were made visible to indicate scrolling

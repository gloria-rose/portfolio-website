About

My Story

I am a UI designer with a background in interdisciplinary research. I have recently started transitioning from the world of academia to UI design, which allows me to continue pursuing a human-centric approach and combine it with creatively expressive design, transforming ideas into appealing, simple and enjoyable products.

People and the way they interact with each other and the world have always been at the centre of my interests, which is why I completed a university education in both prehistoric archaeology (BA) and biological anthropology (MSc) at the University of Vienna.

I greatly enjoy communicating and collaborating with people from different disciplines and backgrounds. I am convinced that there is no better place to be inspired and grow than at these interfaces where different worldviews, experiences and logics come together to form mutual understanding and to create something beautiful, new, or simply more pragmatic.

I spent my formative years in The Netherlands, Oman and the US, and have been based in the wonderful city of Vienna in Austria since 2007.

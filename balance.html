Overview

Balance is a money management app which supports users saving up for specific goals.

My responsibilities in this project encompassed the logo design, logo animation, icon design, the creation of a cohesive design style (color, typography, writing style, imagery), user flows, wireframing (low to high), mid-fidelity prototyping for scenario-based user testing, and creation of the final screens including their prototyping and mockups.

Logo Design

The name Balance refers to both a bank balance and the app's mission of balancing different needs and wants.

The logo is made up of four flat rocks stacked on each other, with each rock representing a goal in the user's life.

The single rocks move in different directions during the logo animation (like changes in life), while staying balanced.

Color Design

I wanted the app to feel reliable and professional, which is why I chose some slightly muted blues. The orange accent color provides a fresh and friendly feeling.

Typography

Poiret One is the logo font. It has an open friendly feeling while also seeming organized and serious.

Dosis is the font of choice for the text elements in the Balance app. It is playful, friendly and legible.

User-Flows

  • As a user I want to create an account to manage my goals and savings
  • As a user I want to have a simple overview over my goal progress and distribution of my savings
  • As a user I want to add new goals with the option to allow automatic deposits from my bank account

Scenario-based Prototype Test

Tests

A mid-fi prototype was created for users to follow the specific scenario instructions. User feedback was documented and served to inform design revisions.

Scenario

You are a new user and would like to sign up and create your first savings goal with automatic deposits. Which steps would you take to accomplish this task?

Feedback-based Revisions

  • More prominent goals
  • Scroll height preserved
  • Revised deposit calculator
  • Revised design of preview page
  • Removed pop-up
